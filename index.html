Got Questions
What is the WEB3BOX ?
WEB3BOX is an innovative Web3 Mystery Box project, funded by top blockchain teams and operated independently in the form of DAO.
What is the WEB3BOX-PASS ?
WEB3BOX-PASS is a pass to purchase the Genesis Mystery Box.
The WEB3BOX system analyzes the data on the blockchain and targets WEB3BOX Pass for high-level users. Only users with WEB3BOX Pass have the right to purchase the Genesis Mystery Box.
This right will be automatically invalidated if it is not used. After 3,000 Genesis Mystery Boxes are sold, WEB3BOX-PASS will be automatically invalidated.
How claim the prizes received in WEB3BOX ?
Users can get various NFTs and Tokens in WEB3BOX Mystery boxes, just go to My WEB3BOX and click "Claim".
Users can claim various rewards to their wallet at any time.
What is the Genesis Mystery Box ?
